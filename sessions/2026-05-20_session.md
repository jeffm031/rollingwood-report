## Key finding — feed the AGENDA, not the packet

The agenda PACKET (251k chars) is ~99% embedded prior-meeting minutes.
Feeding it caused contamination — phantom "participants" (Cooper,
Christopher, Bunch, Howard) and a leaked resident address, all sourced
from embedded minutes rather than the recording.

A "Scope of the packet" prompt rule intended to suppress this BACKFIRED
(bleed grew 2 → 4, and it nicked the names-roster rule) and was reverted.

The plain AGENDA PDF (`MEET-Agenda-<guid>`, same guid as the packet) is
the right input: OCR'd it's 7,625 chars, contains full consent
itemization (items 2–5) and agenda items 6–18, zero embedded minutes =
zero contamination. BUT the agenda PDF is image-only — pypdf and
pdftotext both extract 0 chars. Needs OCR (ocrmypdf/tesseract, installed
via brew this session).
